# Swiss Network User Group (CHNUG)

The Swiss Network User Group (CHNUG) is a community dedicated to exploring all facets of networking. We aim to advance network technology and operations by openly discussing current challenges and sharing best practices. Our meetups offer a platform to discuss a wide range of topics, including, but not limited to, network automation and network observability.

The meetups are open to everyone. Please join us, whether you're at the start of your career or an expert; you can share your experiences and help others on their learning journey.
